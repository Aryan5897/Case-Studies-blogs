## General Image Guidelines

### Image Specifications:
- **Format:** JPG (for photos/complex images) or PNG (for graphics/screenshots)
- **Dimensions:** 1200x630px (optimal for social sharing and blog thumbnails)
- **File Size:** Under 200KB (compressed for web performance)
- **Quality:** High-resolution, crisp, professional

### Style Preferences:
1. **Modern and Clean:** Avoid cluttered or busy designs
2. **Brand-Aligned:** Use company colors where applicable
3. **Contextual:** Images should immediately communicate the topic
4. **Professional:** Suitable for product management/business context
5. **Engaging:** Eye-catching but not overwhelming

### Sources for Images:
- **Stock Photos:** Unsplash, Pexels (free, high-quality)
- **Custom Graphics:** Canva, Figma (for infographics and diagrams)
- **UI Mockups:** Screenshots or recreations of actual products
- **Illustrations:** unDraw, DrawKit (free illustration libraries)
- **Data Viz:** Chart.js visualizations, custom infographics

### AI Image Generation Prompts:
For each case study, you can use AI tools (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion) with these general prompt structures:

**Template:**
"Professional business illustration for [topic], [key visual elements], clean modern design, [color palette], high quality, suitable for blog thumbnail, 16:9 aspect ratio"

**Examples:**

1. **Airbnb Crisis:** "Professional business illustration showing crisis roadmap adaptation during COVID-19, split screen before and after, local travel theme, blue and orange colors, modern design"

2. **NoBroker:** "Professional illustration of Indian real estate marketplace with distribution channels, SEO and referral paths, green and white colors, urban setting, modern digital design"

3. **Perplexity:** "Clean minimalist illustration of AI search with citations, trust and transparency theme, dark blue and white colors, modern tech aesthetic"

4. **TikTok Psychology:** "Modern scientific illustration of dopamine reward loop, brain with neural pathways, infinite scroll concept, cyan and pink accent colors, professional design"

5. **YouTube EdTech:** "Professional illustration of online education platform with structured learning paths, global classroom, YouTube red with educational colors, modern design"
