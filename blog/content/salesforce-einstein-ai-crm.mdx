## PM Learnings

### 1. Embed AI in Workflow, Don't Bolt It On

**Separate AI Tool:**
- Context switching
- Low adoption
- Limited value

**Embedded AI:**
- No friction
- High adoption
- Maximum value

**Lesson:** Integration depth matters

### 2. Prediction Must Come with Prescription

**Prediction Only:**
- "This deal is at risk" (now what?)

**Prediction + Prescription:**
- "This deal is at risk â†’ Call executive sponsor"

**Result:** Actionable intelligence

### 3. Transparency Builds Trust

**Black Box:**
- Reps don't trust
- Won't follow guidance

**Explainable:**
- Shows reasoning
- Builds confidence

### 4. Automation Must Respect Human Control

- Reps feel replaced

**Success:** Flexibility wins
