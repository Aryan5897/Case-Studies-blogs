**UPI Failures:**

- Abandonment increases

**Multiple Payment Methods:**

- Lower failure rate
- Instant payment
- Preloaded convenience

**5. Pay on Delivery Option:**

## PM Learnings (Interview-Ready)

### 1. Checkout Drop-Off Is Emotional + Technical

**Emotional Factors:**
- Surprise (fees)
- Confusion (coupons)
- Anxiety (delivery time)
- Frustration (payment failures)

**Technical Factors:**
- Payment gateway reliability
- Form field optimization
- Load time performance

**Lesson:**
Address both dimensions.

### 2. Price Surprises Kill Intent

**Problem:**
Late fee disclosure.

**Solution:**
Show total price early.

**Impact:**
Trust preserved, conversion improved.

### 3. Fewer Choices Convert Better

**Paradox:**
More coupon options = lower conversion.

**Solution:**
Auto-select best coupon.

**Result:**
Decision fatigue eliminated.

### 4. Reliability Is a Growth Feature

**Payment Failures:**
Not just technical issueâ€”growth blocker.

**Investment:**
Better retry flows, alternative methods, clear messaging.

**Outcome:**
Higher conversion, better retention.

### 5. Optimize Repeat Behavior First

**80/20 Rule:**
- 40% of orders = repeats
- 80% conversion rate on repeats

**Strategy:**
Make repeat orders effortless.

**Impact:**
Highest ROI optimization.

### 6. Payments Are Product, Not Plumbing

**Wrong View:**
Payment is backend infrastructure.

**Right View:**
Payment is user experience.

**Implication:**
Invest in payment UX, not just gateway integration.

### 7. Transparency > Persuasion

**Persuasion Tactics:**
- Hidden fees
- Bait and switch
- Pressure tactics

**Transparency:**
- Clear pricing
- Honest ETAs
- Straightforward policies

**Long-Term Winner:**
Transparency builds trust.
