**LinkedIn's Choice:**
Professional value > viral entertainment.

### 2. Reduce Social Risk to Increase Participation

**Psychological Barrier:**

**Product Solution:**
- Comment previews
- Suggested reactions

- Creators supply content

- Invest in creator tools

**Outcome:**
