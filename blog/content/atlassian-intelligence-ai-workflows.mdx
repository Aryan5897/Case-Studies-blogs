## PM Learnings

### 1. B2B AI Must Be Workflow-Native

**Separate AI Tool:**
- Requires behavior change
- Context switching penalty
- Adoption struggles

**Embedded AI:**
- Zero new habits
- Context preserved
- Natural adoption

**Lesson:** Integration > sophistication

### 2. Context Is the Biggest Moat

**Generic AI:**
- Smart but generic
- No domain knowledge
- Commoditizable

**Contextual AI:**
- Project-aware
- Team-aware
- Process-aware

**Moat:** Deep integration hard to replicate

### 3. Enterprise Needs Privacy Guarantees

**Consumer AI:**
- Train on user data
- Privacy secondary

**Enterprise AI:**
- Zero data training
- Compliance mandatory
- Privacy first

**Requirement:** Trust before adoption

### 4. Task Commands Beat Prompts

**Prompt Engineering:**
- Requires skill
- Inconsistent results
- User frustration

**Task Commands:**
- Clear outcomes
- Predictable results
- Easy to use

**Result:** Higher adoption

### 5. Human-in-the-Loop Is Required

**Auto-Execution:**
- Scary for teams
- Trust issues
- Accountability unclear

**Suggestion Mode:**
- Human oversight
- Controllable
- Accountable

**Outcome:** Enterprise confidence

### 6. Measure Productivity, Not AI Usage

**Wrong Metric:**
- % of users trying AI

**Right Metrics:**
- Hours saved per week
- Faster project delivery
- Quality improvement

**Focus:** Business outcomes

### 7. Adoption Beats Sophistication

**Most Advanced AI:**
- Low adoption if complex

**Simple AI:**
- High adoption if useful

**Priority:** Usefulness > impressiveness
