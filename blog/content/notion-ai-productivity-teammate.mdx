## PM Learnings

### 1. AI Works Best When Embedded

**Bolted-On AI:** Separate tool, context switching

**Embedded AI:** Native to workflow, no friction

**Lesson:** Integration > sophistication

### 2. Context Is the Biggest AI Advantage

**Generic AI:** Good answers, no context

**Contextual AI:** Relevant answers, your data

**Moat:** Deep integration hard to replicate

### 3. Design for Tasks, Not Prompts

**Prompt Engineering:** Technical, intimidating

**Task Commands:** Clear, accessible

**Outcome:** Higher adoption, less confusion

### 4. Human-in-the-Loop Builds Trust

**Auto-Execution:** Scary, loss of control

**Suggestion Mode:** Safe, controllable

**Result:** User confidence and adoption

### 5. Adoption Beats Sophistication

**Most Advanced AI:** Low adoption if complex

**Simple AI:** High adoption if useful

**Priority:** Usefulness > impressiveness

### 6. Monetization After Habit

**Immediate Paywall:** Prevents habit formation

**Value First:** Users want to pay after experiencing benefit

**Strategy:** Generous trial â†’ easy upgrade

### 7. AI Should Amplify, Not Replace

**Replacement Mentality:** Threatens users

**Amplification Mentality:** Empowers users

**Notion's Approach:** Make users better, faster
